1. MANUFACTURING PHARMACEUTICAL INDUSTRY

How we help our clients

Committed to health, we help our private clients, investors, by providing them with global and complete solutions in the realization of industrial site construction projects for pharmaceutical production in order to meet the needs and requirements of our clients.

A consortium dedicated to the design, construction, execution and consulting of large-scale projects dedicated to the health sector.

The main objectives of our partners are the design, construction execution and optimization of the pharmaceutical facilities, for proper operation and performance, in accordance with its design. Our partners can work on all dosage forms, oral solids manufacturing lines, injectables (solutions and lyophilization) and many more. An unrivaled experience to face any type of project, whatever their size.

Basically, our leading partners in the market for clean rooms and containment areas, partners in industry and research, from the design to the qualification of the installations.

Different industries and players are associated, with extensive experience in technology process transfer in the pharmaceutical industry or where the client or investor is new to the industry or has no prior experience on manufacturing himself.

Engineering and design: a group of highly qualified technicians, specialized in the advice and management of pharmaceutical factories, structures and civil engineering works and other qualifications.

Validations and start-up of the factory, dedicated to the installation, qualification and validation of equipment used in the pharmaceutical industry.

Our construction services are complemented by highly qualified people with experiences in various projects and forms. We manage all our projects alongside our clients, offering expertise and advice.
